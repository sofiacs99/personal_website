PERSONAL PASSPORT

FOOD & TRAVEL

This mobile application enables users to document their unique travel experiences in a personal and intimate manner for their own record-keeping but at the same time allowing easy sharing with others. Users will document their comments, ratings, images, and notes about a specific tourist location, restaurant, etc. from anywhere in the world and use this app as their personal passport to keep track of their travel.
Software: Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop
Year: 2021
