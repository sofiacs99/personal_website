Sofia Cacho Sousa is a Graphic Designer and multi-disciplinary Artist from Lima, Peru, based in New York City. Sofia is a BFA Undergraduate at Parsons School of Design, The New School, majoring in Communication Design with a minor in Photography. Her body of work consists of Social Media content, Branding, Product & Packaging design, Photography, Editorial work, amongst others. She’s also interested in sculpture, Drawing, and Jewelry making.

Sofia is currently working as a freelance Graphic Designer so feel free to reach out if you are interested in working with her!
