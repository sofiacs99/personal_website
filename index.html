My name is Sofia Cacho-Sousa and I'm a multi-disciplinary artist from Lima, Peru, based in New York City. I'm a Junior studying Communication Design in Parsons School of Design at The New School, with a minor in Photography. My body of work mainly consists of typographical compositions, packaging design, branding, photography, product design, and editorial work. However, I'm also interested in other disciplines such as Sculpture, Drawing, and Jewelry making. I'm currently working as a freelance graphic designer so feel free to reach out to me if you're interested in working with me!
