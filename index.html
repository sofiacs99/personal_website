My name is Sofia Cacho-Sousa, a multi-disciplinary artist from Lima, Peru, based in New York City. I study Communication Design in The New School's Parsons School of Design, and I am minoring in Photography. My work is mainly focused on Graphic Design but I enjoy creating pieces of work within other art disciplines such as Photography, Sculpture, Drawing, and Jewelry making.
