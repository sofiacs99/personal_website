This Publication Identity called “Sabor, Color,
Cultura” is a small book dedicated to Peruvian
Gastronomy, composed of a variety of recipes
and information about ingredients, awards,
chefs, restaurant recommendations, and more.
It conveys an essence of the Peruvian culture
and emphasizes the importance of our food
and its richness within our community.
